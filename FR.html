ABOUT US

WHAT IS CYBERSTUDIO?

The Cyberstudio is a shared coworking space dedicated to the E-sporters, gamers, streamers, and all actors supporting this wave of digital entrepeneurs. We offer different stream setups to diversify the content. We offer a discord group to keep in touch with your fellow entrepeneurs and share your knowledge. We offer twitch media to host your stream and give you more visibility. We offer different services (creation of assets, overlays, ...) to help you level up your career!

WHY CHOOSE CYBERSTUDIO?

We acknowledge that being an E-Sporter, streamer, or podcaster is a real career track with a lot of work behind the screens and no or little support. Why wait till a structure is willing to sign a contract with you when you can have all the needed support from a coworking space where fellow digital entrepreneurs can grow together, exchange knowledge, and share skills? Where you have access to the indispensable hardware instead of investing all your savings, all that, while limiting your technical difficulties! Join our cyber community and start to grow your career!
